How to study this (~1 week out)

Don't read-all-then-test — that's the fluency-illusion trap. Per chapter: learn → immediately self-test → mix older chapters into later sessions (retrieval + spacing + interleaving). NP-completeness is pure reproduce-the-reduction practice, so drill it actively.
