How to study this該怎麼讀 (~1 week out)（剩約一週）

Don't read-all-then-test — that's the fluency-illusion trap. Per chapter: learn → immediately self-test → mix older chapters into later sessions (retrieval + spacing + interleaving). NP-completeness is pure reproduce-the-reduction practice, so drill it actively.別全部讀完才測驗 — 那是 fluency-illusion 的陷阱。每章請：學習 → 立刻自我測驗 → 把舊章節穿插進後面的複習（retrieval + spacing + interleaving）。NP-completeness 純粹就是重現 reduction 的練習，所以要主動操練。
