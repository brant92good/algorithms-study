Channel RoutingChannel Routing（通道佈線）

A chip has pins lined up on the top and bottom of a corridor; wires connecting same-labelled pins must run along horizontal rows. Use as few rows as possible — and A* finds the minimum.晶片上有一條走道，上下兩排排著一堆 pin；要把同編號的 pin 連起來的線得走在水平的橫列上。目標是用越少橫列越好——而 A* 能找出最少的那個。

📄 Source: Alg05 · The Searching Strategies §12 (Channel Routing via A*; Fig. 6-40 / 6-46 / 6-48).

🧠 Solved by:解這題的方法： A* Algorithm

The problem問題是什麼

In VLSI chip layout, a channel is a rectangular corridor between two rows of components. Along its top edge and its bottom edge sit terminals (pins). Each terminal carries a label, a small integer — pins that share a label belong to the same net and must all be electrically connected by wire.在 VLSI 晶片佈局裡，channel 是兩排元件中間一條長方形的走道。它的上緣和下緣排著一些 terminal（也就是 pin）。每個 terminal 都帶一個編號（一個小整數）——編號一樣的 pin 屬於同一個 net，必須用線把它們全部接通。

  • A net = the group of all terminals (top and bottom) bearing the same label. Every net must be wired up.net＝所有（上面和下面）同編號 terminal 的集合。每個 net 都得接起來。
  • A track = one horizontal row inside the channel along which a wire can run. A net's wire runs mostly along a single track, with short vertical "drops" up to its top/bottom pins.track＝channel 裡一條可以走線的水平橫列。一個 net 的線大部分就沿著一條 track 走，再用短短的垂直「下接」連到它上下的 pin。
  • Two nets may share a track only if their horizontal spans don't overlap — otherwise their wires would short together. That is an illegal wiring.兩個 net 要共用一條 track，前提是它們的水平範圍不重疊——不然兩條線會短路在一起，這種佈線是不合法的。

Goal: assign every net to a track so that no two overlapping nets land on the same track, and minimize the total number of tracks. Fewer tracks means a shorter channel and a smaller chip. This problem is NP-complete.目標：把每個 net 分配到某條 track 上，讓任何兩個會重疊的 net 都不落在同一條 track，並且讓 track 的總數最少。track 越少，channel 越短、晶片越小。這題是 NP-complete。

The concrete instance (Fig. 6-40). A channel with 13 columns. Each column lists its top pin and bottom pin (0 = no pin):具體的例子（Fig. 6-40）。一條有 13 行（column）的 channel。每一行列出它上面的 pin 和下面的 pin（0 = 沒有 pin）：

So net 1 spans from column 11 (top) to column 13 (bottom); net 7 from column 4 (top) to column 3 (bottom); net 8 from column 2 (top) to column 8 (bottom); and so on. A naïve feasible routing needs 7 tracks (one net each); the optimal routing fits everything into just 4 tracks.所以 net 1 的範圍從第 11 行（上）跨到第 13 行（下）；net 7 從第 4 行（上）到第 3 行（下）；net 8 從第 2 行（上）到第 8 行（下），以此類推。隨便來個可行解要用 7 條 track（每個 net 各占一條）；但最佳佈線只要 4 條 track 就全塞得下。

Two helper graphs capture the constraints.用兩張輔助 graph 把限制條件抓出來。

  • The Horizontal Constraint Graph (HCG): a node per net; a directed edge u → v means "if nets u and v ever share a track, u must sit to the left of v" — i.e. their spans conflict. Two nets joined in the HCG cannot freely overlap. A set of nets that mutually conflict is a clique; the maximal cliques here are {1,8}, {1,3,7}, and {5,7}. Each maximal clique is a bundle of nets that can be packed onto one track.Horizontal Constraint Graph（HCG，水平限制圖）：每個 net 一個節點；一條有向邊 u → v 的意思是「如果 net u 和 v 共用一條 track，u 一定要在 v 的左邊」——也就是它們的範圍有衝突。在 HCG 裡連起來的兩個 net 不能隨便重疊。一組彼此都衝突的 net 叫做一個 clique；這裡的 maximal clique 是 {1,8}、{1,3,7}、{5,7}。每個 maximal clique 就是一束可以塞進同一條 track 的 net。
  • The Vertical Constraint Graph (VCG): an edge top-net → bottom-net wherever a column has one net on top and another on the bottom, forcing the top net into a track above the bottom net. Here the VCG is just 1→2, 3→4, 5→6 (e.g. "net 3 must be wired before net 4"); nets 7 and 8 are unconstrained vertically.Vertical Constraint Graph（VCG，垂直限制圖）：只要某一行上面是一個 net、下面是另一個 net，就拉一條邊 上面的net → 下面的net，逼上面那個 net 走的 track 要在下面那個 net 上方。這裡的 VCG 就只有 1→2、3→4、5→6（例如「net 3 要排在 net 4 上面」）；net 7 和 8 在垂直方向沒有限制。

Watch it solved看它怎麼解

First the HCG of this channel (nodes = nets, arrows = "must be left of"); the three maximal cliques are the candidate first tracks. Then A* searches the tree of track assignments with f = g + h, where g = the tree level (tracks committed so far) and h = the maximal local density (a lower bound on tracks still needed, so it never overestimates → A* is optimal). Step through.先看這條 channel 的 HCG（節點 = net，箭頭 = 「必須在左邊」）；那三個 maximal clique 就是第一條 track 的候選。接著 A* 用 f = g + h 去搜尋 track 分配的樹，其中 g = 樹的層數（目前已用掉的 track 數），h = maximal local density（還需要多少 track 的下界，所以絕不高估 → A* 會是最佳的）。一步步看下去。
