Pack the most valuable load into a fixed-capacity bag when each item is all-or-nothing — solved by building a table of answers to smaller bags.在容量固定的袋子裡裝進總價值最高的東西，而且每樣物品都是要嘛整個拿、要嘛不拿——靠著建一張「小袋子答案」的表來解。

📄 Source: Alg07 · Ch 7 §6–7, §12 (0/1 Knapsack by DP), slide 30.

🧠 Solved by: Dynamic Programming (also by Branch-and-Bound)

The problem這題在問什麼

You have a knapsack (a bag) that can carry at most M units of weight. There are n items; item i has a weight wᵢ and a profit (value) pᵢ. You must choose a set of items whose total weight fits in the bag and whose total profit is as large as possible.你有一個 knapsack（背包），最多裝得下 M 單位的重量。有 n 樣物品；第 i 樣有 weight wᵢ 和 profit（價值）pᵢ。你要挑一組物品，總重量塞得進背包，而且總 profit 越大越好。

The "0/1" means each item is taken whole or not at all — you can't slice an item to take half of it. So for every item the decision is a single yes/no: xᵢ = 1 (take it) or xᵢ = 0 (skip it). Formally, maximize Σ pᵢxᵢ subject to Σ wᵢxᵢ ≤ M with each xᵢ ∈ {0,1}.「0/1」的意思是每樣物品要嘛整個拿、要嘛完全不拿——不能切一半。所以每樣物品的決定就是一個 yes/no：xᵢ = 1（拿）或 xᵢ = 0（不拿）。正式講就是：在 Σ wᵢxᵢ ≤ M 且每個 xᵢ ∈ {0,1} 的條件下，最大化 Σ pᵢxᵢ。

A tiny concrete instance. Four items, written as (weight, profit) pairs: (1,1), (3,4), (4,5), (5,7), and a bag of capacity M = 7. By hand you can check that taking items 2 and 3 — weights 3+4 = 7 (exactly fills the bag) for profit 4+5 = 9 — beats every other feasible choice. That 9 is the answer we want a method to find without trying all 2ⁿ subsets.來個小例子。四樣物品，用 (weight, profit) 表示：(1,1)、(3,4)、(4,5)、(5,7)，背包容量 M = 7。手算就能驗證，拿物品 2 和 3——重量 3+4 = 7（剛好塞滿背包），profit 4+5 = 9——贏過其他所有可行的選法。這個 9 就是我們想找的答案，而且要不必把 2ⁿ 種子集合全試一遍。

The method (dynamic programming). Look at the items one at a time and split on the next decision. For the next item you either skip it (the bag is unchanged) or take it (the bag now has wᵢ less room, and you've banked pᵢ profit). Either way, what's left is the same kind of problem on the remaining items — a subproblem. DP solves every subproblem once and stores the answers in a table so they're never recomputed.解法（dynamic programming）。一次看一樣物品，從下一個決定切下去。對下一樣物品，你要嘛不拿（背包沒變），要嘛拿（背包少了 wᵢ 空間，但你賺到了 pᵢ profit）。不管哪種，剩下的都是針對其餘物品的同一類問題——也就是一個 subproblem（子問題）。DP 把每個子問題只解一次，把答案存進表裡，這樣就不會重算。

Mind the direction — this is the opposite of what most people guess. The subscript is how many items you've already passed, not how many you can use. So g₀ sees all four items (items 1…4) and is the final answer; g₄ sees no items and is the base. In general gᵢ only knows about items numbered higher than i. That is why we will build the table from the bottom row g₄ upward to g₀, and why "adding item 1" happens in the top row g₀, not the first.注意方向——這跟大多數人直覺猜的剛好相反。下標代表的是你已經跳過幾樣物品，不是你還能用幾樣。所以 g₀ 看得到全部四樣（物品 1…4），它是最終答案；g₄ 一樣都看不到，是 base。一般來說，gᵢ 只認得編號大於 i 的物品。這就是為什麼我們會從最底下的 g₄ 那一列往上建到 g₀，也是為什麼「加進物品 1」發生在最上面的 g₀ 列，而不是第一列。

g₄ = {} (base) → g₃ sees {4} → g₂ sees {3,4} → g₁ sees {2,3,4} → g₀ sees {1,2,3,4} (answer)

gᵢ(y) = max{ gi+1(y) ,  gi+1(y − wi+1) + pi+1 }

The left term is skip item i+1; the right term is take it (only allowed when y ≥ wi+1). The base case is gₙ(y) = 0 for every y (no items left ⇒ no profit), and the final answer is g₀(M).左邊那項是不拿物品 i+1；右邊那項是拿它（只有 y ≥ wi+1 時才允許）。base case 是對每個 y 都有 gₙ(y) = 0（沒物品了 ⇒ 沒 profit），最終答案是 g₀(M)。

For our instance we build a table of gᵢ(y), one row per i (from the empty base row g₄ up to g₀) and one column per capacity y = 0…7.針對這個例子，我們建一張 gᵢ(y) 表，每個 i 一列（從空的 base 列 g₄ 往上到 g₀），每個容量 y = 0…7 一欄。

Three views of this problem: 0/1 Knapsack appears three ways in this course: (1) Branch-and-Bound (Ch 6, Fig 6-28) — search the decision tree, pruning with bounds; (2) this DP recurrence; (3) as a multistage graph (Ch 7 §12), where each stage is one item and each path is a packing.這題的三種看法：0/1 Knapsack 在這門課出現了三次：(1) Branch-and-Bound（Ch 6, Fig 6-28）——搜尋決策樹，用 bound 來剪枝；(2) 這個 DP 遞迴式；(3) 當成一張 multistage graph（Ch 7 §12），每個 stage 是一樣物品，每條路徑就是一種裝法。

Watch it solved看它怎麼解

Each row is computed from the row below it. The gold cell is the one being filled; the amber cells are its two sources — skip = same column one row down, take = column y−w one row down, plus p.每一列都是從它下面那一列算出來的。金色格是正在填的；琥珀色格是它的兩個來源——不拿 = 下一列同一欄，拿 = 下一列的 y−w 欄，再加 p。

Where the cost comes from. The table has n+1 rows (one per item-index, here 5: g₀…g₄) and M+1 columns (one per capacity, here 8: y=0…7). Each cell is filled by one max of two already-computed cells — that's O(1) work. Multiply rows × columns × cost-per-cell and you get O(nM) time, and since we store every cell, O(nM) space too.複雜度是怎麼來的。這張表有 n+1 列（每個 item-index 一列，這裡是 5 列：g₀…g₄），M+1 欄（每個容量一欄，這裡是 8 欄：y=0…7）。每一格只要對兩個已經算好的格子取一次 max——就是 O(1) 的工。列 × 欄 × 每格成本乘起來，就得到 O(nM) 時間；又因為每一格都存下來，空間也是 O(nM)。

Why "pseudo-polynomial"? O(nM) looks polynomial, but watch the M: it's a numeric value (the capacity), not the length of the input. Writing the number M only takes about log M digits, yet the runtime grows with M itself — so if someone hands you a bag of capacity M = 1,000,000,000, the table explodes even though the input barely changed size. An algorithm that is polynomial in the numeric values but not in the input length is called pseudo-polynomial.為什麼叫「pseudo-polynomial」？O(nM) 看起來像多項式，但注意那個 M：它是一個數值（容量），不是輸入的長度。把數字 M 寫出來只需要大約 log M 位數，可是執行時間卻是隨 M 本身成長——所以如果給你一個容量 M = 1,000,000,000 的背包，輸入幾乎沒變大，表卻爆掉了。對數值是多項式、但對輸入長度不是的演算法，就叫 pseudo-polynomial。

Reading back which items (the rule, not just this example). The table gives the best profit, but to recover the actual packing you walk back down through it. Start at the answer cell g₀(M) and at each row gᵢ(y) ask one question: does the value equal gi+1(y)? If yes, item i+1 was skipped — drop to the next row keeping the same capacity y. If not, the value must have come from the take branch, so item i+1 was taken — record it, subtract its weight (move to column y − wi+1), and drop to the next row. Repeat until you reach the base row. (When both branches give the same value the item is genuinely optional; either choice is optimal.) Run this rule on our table and you get exactly: skip 1, take 2, take 3, skip 4 — the load shown in the last frame above.怎麼倒推出到底拿了哪些物品（這是通則，不只適用這個例子）。表給的是最佳 profit，但想知道實際的裝法，要沿著表往回走。從答案格 g₀(M) 出發，每到一列 gᵢ(y) 就問一個問題：這格的值，是不是等於 gi+1(y)？如果是，代表物品 i+1 被跳過——容量 y 不變，往下一列走。如果不是，那這個值一定來自「拿」那一支，所以物品 i+1 是拿了——把它記下來，扣掉它的重量（移到 y − wi+1 欄），再往下一列走。一直重複到 base 列為止。（如果兩支算出來一樣大，那這個物品拿不拿都行，兩種都是最佳解。）把這個規則套到我們的表上，剛好就會得到：skip 1、take 2、take 3、skip 4——也就是上面最後一格顯示的那種裝法。

Time: O(nM) Space: O(nM) Class: pseudo-polynomial
