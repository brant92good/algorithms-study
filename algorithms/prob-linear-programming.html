2-Variable Linear Programming2-Variable Linear Programming

Given a list of straight-line constraints, find the single point (x, y) that makes a linear objective as small as possible — the lowest point of a convex "valley".給你一堆直線形式的限制條件，要找出那一個點 (x, y)，讓某個線性目標值越小越好——也就是一個凸「山谷」的最低點。

📄 Source: Alg06 · §6 (Two-Variable Linear Programming), Fig 7-2 (the F(x) envelope), Fig 7-6 (slope cases).

🧠 Solved by:解法： Prune-and-Search

The problem這題在問什麼

You have two unknowns, x and y — together they pick a single point in the plane. You want to make a linear objective as small as possible. "Linear" means the objective is just a weighted sum like a·x + b·y; you minimize it. That choice isn't free: it's fenced in by a list of n linear constraints, each an inequality of the form aᵢ·x + bᵢ·y ≤ cᵢ. The general statement is:你有兩個未知數 x 跟 y，它們合起來就是平面上的一個點。你要讓某個線性目標越小越好。「線性」的意思就是目標只是個加權和，像 a·x + b·y 這樣，你要把它最小化。但這個選擇不是隨便挑：它被 n 條線性限制框住，每一條都是 aᵢ·x + bᵢ·y ≤ cᵢ 這種不等式。一般的寫法是：

Minimize  a·x + b·y    subject to   aᵢ·x + bᵢ·y ≤ cᵢ ,   i = 1 … n

Some vocabulary, defined from zero:幾個名詞，從頭講清楚：

  • A constraint aᵢx + bᵢy ≤ cᵢ is a half-plane — it splits the plane with a straight line and keeps everything on one side.一條限制 aᵢx + bᵢy ≤ cᵢ 就是一個 half-plane（半平面）——用一條直線把平面切開，只留下其中一邊。
  • The feasible region is the set of points satisfying all n constraints at once: the intersection of all the half-planes. It is a convex polygon (possibly unbounded).feasible region（可行區域）就是同時滿足全部 n 條限制的點集合：所有 half-plane 的交集。它是一個凸多邊形（可能是無界的）。
  • The optimum is the feasible point with the smallest objective value. We call it (x₀, y₀).optimum（最佳解）就是可行區域裡目標值最小的那個點，我們叫它 (x₀, y₀)。

The canonical, easy-to-picture version.最好想像的標準版本。 We can reduce the general problem to a tidy special case where you simply minimize y. This is just a change of coordinates, so nothing is lost — let's do it concretely. Introduce a new vertical coordinate我們可以把一般問題化簡成一個乾淨的特例，讓你只要最小化 y。這其實只是換座標，所以資訊完全沒有損失——我們一步步做給你看。先引入一個新的縱座標

y' = a·x + b·y

That y' is exactly the objective we wanted to minimize. So in the new (x, y') picture the goal "minimize a·x + b·y" literally becomes "minimize y'" — push the point as low as possible vertically. (Assuming b ≠ 0; if b = 0 the problem is 1-D and trivial.) Now rewrite a constraint. Solve y' = a·x + b·y for the old y, namely y = (y' − a·x)/b, and plug it into a constraint aᵢ·x + bᵢ·y ≤ cᵢ; collecting terms turns it into a bound of the form y' ≥ aᵢ'·x + bᵢ' (a fresh line in the new coordinates). Renaming y' back to y and the new coefficients back to aᵢ, bᵢ, every rule is now "the point must lie on or above this line":這個 y' 剛好就是我們本來要最小化的目標。所以換到新的 (x, y') 圖上，「最小化 a·x + b·y」就直接變成「最小化 y'」——也就是把點在縱向壓到越低越好。（這裡假設 b ≠ 0；如果 b = 0 那其實是 1 維問題，很簡單。）接著改寫限制：把 y' = a·x + b·y 解出舊的 y，也就是 y = (y' − a·x)/b，代回某條限制 aᵢ·x + bᵢ·y ≤ cᵢ，整理之後會變成 y' ≥ aᵢ'·x + bᵢ' 這種形式（在新座標裡的一條新線）。把 y' 改回叫 y、新係數改回叫 aᵢ, bᵢ，每條規則就都變成「點必須落在這條線上或上方」：

Minimize  y    subject to   y ≥ aᵢ·x + bᵢ ,   i = 1 … n

Each rule y ≥ aᵢx + bᵢ says the point must sit on or above that line. With n rules, the floor you must stay above at each x is the highest of all the lines there — the pointwise maximum, called the upper envelope:每條規則 y ≥ aᵢx + bᵢ 都在說：點必須落在那條線上或上方。有 n 條規則時，每個 x 處你必須踩在上面的那個地板，就是當地最高的那條線——也就是逐點取最大值，叫做 upper envelope（上包絡）：

F(x) = max { a₁x+b₁, a₂x+b₂, …, aₙx+bₙ }

Because it is the max of straight lines, F is a convex "valley": it descends, reaches one bottom, and climbs again — it can never dip twice. So the whole problem collapses to: find the lowest point of F. The answer is that x₀, with y₀ = F(x₀).因為它是一堆直線取最大值，F 是一個 convex（凸）的「山谷」：它先往下走，到一個最低點，再往上爬——絕不會凹兩次。所以整題就濃縮成一句話：找出 F 的最低點。答案就是那個 x₀，搭配 y₀ = F(x₀)。

What does "the slope of F" mean? Since F is the max of several lines, it is piecewise-linear — at each x exactly one line is on top (the highest one, the active line), and F there is just that line, so the slope of F at x is the slope aᵢ of whichever line is currently highest. As x increases, the active line keeps changing, so the slope changes from piece to piece — that's why we always say "the slope of F at this point", never "the slope of F" as one number. At the bottom of the valley two lines cross: a line of negative slope (the left wall) meets one of positive slope (the right wall). That corner is the optimum x₀ — to its left F goes down, to its right it goes up. (If a flat aᵢ = 0 line is the lowest at the bottom, F is briefly flat there and any point on the flat is optimal.)「F 的斜率」是什麼意思？ 因為 F 是好幾條線取最大值，它是分段線性的——每個 x 恰好只有一條線在最上面（最高的那條，也就是active 的那條），F 在那裡其實就等於那條線，所以 F 在某個 x 的斜率，就是當下最高那條線的斜率 aᵢ。當 x 變大，最高的線會一直換，斜率也就一段一段跟著變——這就是為什麼我們永遠講「F 在這一點的斜率」，而不會把「F 的斜率」當成單一個數。在谷底，兩條線會交會：一條負斜率（左牆）碰上一條正斜率（右牆），那個轉角就是最佳解 x₀——它左邊 F 往下、右邊往上。（如果谷底最低的是一條 aᵢ = 0 的水平線，那 F 在那邊會有一小段是平的，平段上任何一點都是最佳解。）

Tiny concrete instance.來個小例子。 Three constraints y ≥ 2, y ≥ x, y ≥ −x + 4. At x = 1 the three lines give 2, 1, 3, so F(1) = max{2,1,3} = 3. Sweeping x, the valley bottoms out at x = 2 where lines y = x and y = −x + 4 cross at height 2 — there the optimum is (x₀, y₀) = (2, 2).三條限制 y ≥ 2、y ≥ x、y ≥ −x + 4。在 x = 1 時三條線給出 2、1、3，所以 F(1) = max{2,1,3} = 3。讓 x 掃過去，山谷在 x = 2 觸底——這裡 y = x 跟 y = −x + 4 剛好在高度 2 交會，最佳解就是 (x₀, y₀) = (2, 2)。

How the prune-and-search actually worksprune-and-search 到底怎麼運作

Finding the bottom of F by checking all n lines every time would be slow. The trick is to throw away a constant fraction of the lines in each round while still keeping the optimum, so the work shrinks geometrically (n + n/2 + n/4 + … = O(n)). Here is one round, step by step, with the actual mechanics — no hand-waving.每次都檢查全部 n 條線去找 F 的谷底太慢了。訣竅是每一輪都丟掉固定比例的線，又保證最佳解還在裡面，這樣工作量會等比縮小（n + n/2 + n/4 + … = O(n)）。下面把一輪拆開、用真正的機制講清楚——不打迷糊仗。

  1. Pair the lines. Take the n lines two at a time, forming n/2 pairs. Why pair? Because two lines either are parallel or cross at exactly one x, and that single crossing is the only place their relative order (which one is higher) flips. Knowing which side of the optimum that crossing is on will let us decide which line of the pair is useless.把線兩兩配對。把 n 條線兩條一組，配成 n/2 對。為什麼要配對？因為兩條線要嘛平行、要嘛恰好在某個 x 交會一次，而那個唯一的交點，正是它們高低順序唯一會翻轉的地方。只要知道那個交點在最佳解的哪一邊，就能判斷這一對裡哪一條沒用。
  2. Take the median of the crossings as xₘ. Each pair gives one crossing x-value, so you have n/2 numbers. Compute their median (in O(n) by linear-time selection) and call it xₘ. Why the median? Because exactly half of the crossings sit on each side of xₘ. Whichever side of xₘ the optimum turns out to be on, the crossings on the far side — about n/4 of them — are the ones we can act on.取這些交點的中位數當 xₘ。每一對給一個交點 x 值，所以你有 n/2 個數。算它們的中位數（用 linear-time selection，O(n)），叫它 xₘ。為什麼取中位數？因為這樣交點恰好有一半落在 xₘ 兩邊。不管最佳解最後在 xₘ 哪一邊，落在另一邊（遠側）的那些交點——大約 n/4 個——就是我們能下手的對象。
  3. Slope test at xₘ. Evaluate F(xₘ) (one pass over the lines, O(n)), find the active line there, and read its slope. The full rule is: slope down (negative) ⇒ optimum is to the RIGHT; slope up (positive) ⇒ optimum is to the LEFT; flat (slopes of opposite sign meet at xₘ) ⇒ xₘ is the optimum, stop. This works because F is a convex valley, so its slope is increasing — negative before the bottom, positive after.在 xₘ 做斜率測試。算出 F(xₘ)（掃一遍所有線，O(n)），找出當地的 active 線，讀它的斜率。完整規則是：斜率往下（負）⇒ 最佳解在右邊；斜率往上（正）⇒ 最佳解在左邊；剛好變平（兩條異號斜率的線在 xₘ 交會）⇒ xₘ 就是最佳解，收工。會這樣是因為 F 是凸山谷，斜率是遞增的——谷底前是負的、谷底後是正的。
  4. Prune one line per pair on the wrong side. Say the test says the optimum is to the left of xₘ. Look at any pair whose crossing is to the right of xₘ (these are the far-side ones, about n/4 pairs). To the left of that crossing — which includes the entire region where the optimum could be — one fixed line of the pair stays below the other the whole time. The lower one can never be the highest line there, so it can never be on the upper envelope F anywhere the optimum lives. That line is safe to delete. (Concretely: of the two, keep the one with the smaller slope if the optimum is to the left, the larger slope if to the right — to the left of the crossing the smaller-slope line sits on top, to the right the larger-slope one does, and "on top" is the only part that can touch the envelope where the optimum lives.) One safe deletion per far-side pair removes ~n/4 lines, and the optimum is provably untouched. Repeat with the survivors.每個站錯邊的配對砍掉一條線。假設測試說最佳解在 xₘ 的左邊。看那些交點落在 xₘ 右邊的配對（這些就是遠側的，大約 n/4 對）。在那個交點的左邊——這整段正好涵蓋了最佳解可能在的所有位置——這一對裡有一條固定地一直壓在另一條下面。下面那條在這裡永遠當不了最高的線，所以在最佳解所在的範圍內，它不可能出現在 upper envelope F 上。這條線可以放心刪掉。（具體講：兩條之中，最佳解在左就留斜率小的、在右就留斜率大的——交點左邊是斜率小的那條在上面、右邊是斜率大的那條在上面，而最佳解所在的那一側，只有「在上面」的那條才碰得到 envelope。）每個遠側配對安全刪一條，就拿掉了~n/4 條線，而且可以證明最佳解完全沒被動到。再對活下來的線重複同樣的步驟。

Each round costs O(n) (pairing, median, one envelope evaluation, the deletions) and kills a constant fraction of the lines, so the totals form a shrinking geometric series summing to O(n). That linear-time bound is the whole point of prune-and-search.每一輪花 O(n)（配對、取中位數、算一次 envelope、刪線），又砍掉固定比例的線，所以總和是一個會縮小的等比級數，加起來是 O(n)。這個線性時間就是 prune-and-search 的精髓。
