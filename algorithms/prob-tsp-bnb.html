Travelling Salesperson — Branch & Bound旅行推銷員問題 — Branch & Bound

Given a table of city-to-city travel costs, find the cheapest round trip that visits every city exactly once and returns home — proven optimal without trying all the orderings.給你一張城市之間的旅費表，找出一條最便宜的環遊路線：每個城市都剛好經過一次再繞回原點。而且我們會證明它是最佳的，卻不必把所有排列都試過一遍。

🧠 Solved by:用什麼解： Branch-and-Bound

The problem問題是什麼

A salesperson must visit n cities and come back to where they started. We're given an n×n cost matrix where entry (i, j) is the cost of travelling from city i directly to city j (the diagonal is ∞ — you can't go from a city to itself). A tour is an order in which you visit all the cities, ending back at the start; its cost is the sum of the arc costs along the way.一個推銷員要跑遍 n 個城市再回到出發點。我們有一張 n×n 的成本矩陣，第 (i, j) 格是從城市 i 直接到城市 j 的旅費（對角線是 ∞——你不能從一個城市走到自己）。一條 tour 就是把所有城市走完再回到起點的順序，它的成本就是沿途每段 arc 的旅費加總。

The task:任務： find the tour of minimum total cost. This is the Travelling Salesperson Problem (TSP), and it is NP-complete — there are (n−1)! possible tours, so brute force is hopeless even for modest n.找出總成本最小的那條 tour。這就是 Travelling Salesperson Problem (TSP)，它是 NP-complete——可能的 tour 有 (n−1)! 條，所以就算 n 不大，硬爆搜也沒救。

The concrete instance (Table 6-3).實際例子（Table 6-3）。 Seven cities, with this cost matrix (rows = "from", columns = "to", ∞ on the diagonal):七個城市，成本矩陣如下（列＝「from」、行＝「to」，對角線是 ∞）：

to: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 from 1: ∞ 3 93 13 33 9 57 from 2: 4 ∞ 77 42 21 16 34 from 3: 45 17 ∞ 36 16 28 25 from 4: 39 90 80 ∞ 56 7 91 from 5: 28 46 88 33 ∞ 25 57 from 6: 3 88 18 46 92 ∞ 7 from 7: 44 26 33 27 84 39 ∞

We want the cheapest tour through all 7 cities. The answer turns out to be the tour 1→4→6→7→3→5→2→1 at cost 126 — but the interesting part is how Branch-and-Bound proves no cheaper tour exists without enumerating all 6! = 720 orderings.我們要找跑遍這 7 個城市最便宜的 tour。答案是 1→4→6→7→3→5→2→1，成本 126——不過有趣的是 Branch-and-Bound 怎麼證明沒有更便宜的 tour，卻不用把全部 6! = 720 種排列都列出來。

First, two words you'll see everywhere.先講兩個整頁都會用到的詞。 An arc is a single directed step i→j — one leg of the trip, the move from city i straight to city j. A subtour is a small loop that closes before you've visited every city (e.g. 4→6→4). A real tour is one big loop through all 7 cities; any small premature loop is illegal, so when a choice would create one we forbid the arc that closes it.一個 arc 就是一個有方向的步驟 i→j——旅程的一段，從城市 i 直接走到城市 j。一個 subtour 是還沒走完所有城市就提早繞回去、形成的小圈圈（例如 4→6→4）。真正的 tour 是穿過全部 7 個城市的一個大圈；任何提早閉合的小圈都是違法的，所以只要某個選擇會生出小圈，我們就把閉合它的那條 arc 禁掉。

The key tool — a lower bound from the reduced cost matrix.關鍵工具——從 reduced cost matrix 算出來的下界。 Every tour must leave each city once, so it pays at least the cheapest exit from each city. Subtract each row's minimum from that row (now every row has a zero), then subtract each column's minimum from any column still without a zero. The total amount subtracted is cost that any tour is forced to pay — a valid lower bound. For this instance the row reductions sum to 84 and the column reductions add 7+1+4 = 12, for a root lower bound of 96.每條 tour 都得從每個城市離開一次，所以它至少要付掉每個城市最便宜的那條出去的路。先把每列減掉該列的最小值（這樣每列都有一個 0），再把每行減掉該行的最小值（只挑還沒有 0 的行）。減掉的總量就是任何 tour 都躲不掉的成本——一個有效的下界。這個例子裡，列的 reduction 加起來是 84，行的 reduction 再加 7+1+4 = 12，根節點下界就是 96。

Let's actually do the reduction (so 84 and 7+1+4 aren't magic). Step 1 — row mins. Each row's smallest entry, subtracted from the whole row: row 1 min is 3 (the arc 1→2), row 2 is 4, row 3 is 16, row 4 is 7 (the arc 4→6!), row 5 is 25, row 6 is 3, row 7 is 26. Sum = 3+4+16+7+25+3+26 = 84. After this every row has a 0 where its cheapest exit was — e.g. row 1's 3 became 0 at 1→2, and row 4's 7 became 0 at 4→6.我們真的把 reduction 算一遍（這樣 84 跟 7+1+4 就不是憑空冒出來的）。步驟 1——列的最小值。每一列挑出最小的那格，整列減掉：第 1 列最小是 3（arc 1→2），第 2 列是 4，第 3 列是 16，第 4 列是 7（就是 arc 4→6！），第 5 列是 25，第 6 列是 3，第 7 列是 26。加起來 3+4+16+7+25+3+26 = 84。減完之後每一列在原本最便宜的出口都變成 0——例如第 1 列的 3 在 1→2 變 0，第 4 列的 7 在 4→6 變 0。

Step 2 — column mins. Now look down each column: most already contain a 0, but three don't. Column 3 ("to city 3") has no zero — its smallest remaining entry is 7, so subtract 7. Column 4's smallest is 1 → subtract 1. Column 7's smallest is 4 → subtract 4. That's the 7+1+4 = 12. (Columns 1, 2, 5, 6 already had a zero, so they contribute nothing.) Grand total subtracted = 84 + 12 = 96. Here is the fully reduced matrix — notice every row and every column now contains at least one 0:步驟 2——行的最小值。接著看每一行（column）：大部分已經有 0 了，但有三行沒有。第 3 行（「to city 3」）沒有 0——剩下最小的是 7，減 7。第 4 行最小是 1 → 減 1。第 7 行最小是 4 → 減 4。這就是 7+1+4 = 12。（第 1、2、5、6 行本來就有 0，所以不用減。）總共減掉 84 + 12 = 96。下面是完全 reduce 過的矩陣——你會發現現在每一列和每一行都至少有一個 0：

to: 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 from 1: ∞ 0 83 9 30 6 50 from 2: 0 ∞ 66 37 17 12 26 from 3: 29 1 ∞ 19 0 12 5 from 4: 32 83 66 ∞ 49 0 80 from 5: 3 21 56 7 ∞ 0 28 from 6: 0 85 8 42 89 ∞ 0 from 7: 18 0 0 0 58 13 ∞

A 0 in cell (i,j) means "city i can take its cheapest exit by going to j at no extra cost beyond the 96 we've already counted." Those zeros are exactly the arcs we'll consider committing to.某格 (i,j) 是 0，意思是「城市 i 走去 j 就是它最便宜的出口，除了已經算進去的 96 以外不用再多付」。這些 0 正好就是我們接下來會考慮「選進來」的那些 arc。

How a node splits — and why each bound is what it is一個節點怎麼分裂——還有每個下界為什麼是那個數字

Branch-and-Bound picks one promising arc and splits the world in two: the subtree where we include it (commit to using that arc) and the subtree where we exclude it (ban it). Each side gets its own lower bound. The two bounds are computed by two different little rules — here they both are, fully worked, so every number in the tree below is checkable.Branch-and-Bound 每次挑一條有潛力的 arc，把問題劈成兩半：一邊是選它（include）（一定要用這條 arc）、另一邊是不選它（exclude）（禁掉它）。兩邊各自有自己的下界。這兩個下界是用兩條不同的小規則算的——下面把它們各完整算一遍，這樣後面樹裡的每個數字你都能對得起來。

Exclude rule (the penalty).Exclude 規則（penalty）。 If we ban a zero-cost arc i→j, city i must now leave some other way and city j must be entered some other way. The cheapest forced detour is: penalty = (smallest entry left in row i, ignoring column j) + (smallest entry left in column j, ignoring row i). The excluded node's bound = parent bound + penalty. Worked for arc 4→6: set cell (4,6) to ∞. The smallest other entry in row 4 is 32 (at 4→1); the smallest other entry in column 6 is 0 (at 5→6 or 1→6). So penalty = 32 + 0 = 32, and the "without 4-6" bound = 96 + 32 = 128.如果我們禁掉一條成本 0 的 arc i→j，城市 i 現在得換別條路出去，而且城市 j 也得換別條路進來。最便宜的被迫繞路就是：penalty =（第 i 列剩下、不算第 j 行的最小值）+（第 j 行剩下、不算第 i 列的最小值）。被排除節點的下界 = 父節點下界 + penalty。以 arc 4→6 為例：把 (4,6) 設成 ∞。第 4 列其他格最小是 32（在 4→1）；第 6 行其他格最小是 0（在 5→6 或 1→6）。所以 penalty = 32 + 0 = 32，「without 4-6」的下界 = 96 + 32 = 128。

Why branch on 4→6 and not, say, 3→5? We compute the exclude-penalty of every zero and branch on the largest one, because a big penalty means "banning this arc is very expensive, so it's almost certainly in the answer — committing to it is safe and forces the most progress." Arc 3→5's penalty is only +18 (row-3 next-best 1 + column-5 next-best 17), while 4→6 gives +32. Bigger penalty wins, so we split on 4→6.為什麼挑 4→6 來 branch，而不是 3→5？我們把每個 0 的 exclude-penalty 都算出來，挑最大的那個來 branch。因為 penalty 大代表「禁掉這條 arc 超貴」，那它幾乎一定在答案裡——把它選進來很安全，又能逼出最多進度。arc 3→5 的 penalty 只有 +18（第 3 列次小 1 + 第 5 行次小 17），而 4→6 是 +32。penalty 大的贏，所以我們對 4→6 下刀。

You don't have to take "4→6 is the largest" on faith — there are only 10 zeros in the reduced matrix, and you can score every one with the same penalty rule (next-best left in its row + next-best left in its column). Here is the full scoreboard, so the winner is checkable: 1→2 = 6+0 = 6, 2→1 = 12+0 = 12, 3→5 = 1+17 = 18, 4→6 = 32+0 = 32, 5→6 = 3+0 = 3, 6→1 = 0+0 = 0, 6→7 = 0+5 = 5, 7→2 = 0+0 = 0, 7→3 = 0+8 = 8, 7→4 = 0+7 = 7. The biggest is 4→6 at 32, so that's the arc we split on.「4→6 最大」這件事你不用硬信——reduced matrix 裡只有 10 個 0，每一個都可以用同一條 penalty 規則打分（該列剩下次小 + 該行剩下次小）。下面把整張記分表都列出來，這樣冠軍就驗得出來：1→2 = 6+0 = 6、2→1 = 12+0 = 12、3→5 = 1+17 = 18、4→6 = 32+0 = 32、5→6 = 3+0 = 3、6→1 = 0+0 = 0、6→7 = 0+5 = 5、7→2 = 0+0 = 0、7→3 = 0+8 = 8、7→4 = 0+7 = 7。最大的是 4→6 的 32，所以我們就挑這條 arc 下刀。

Include rule (delete + re-reduce).Include 規則（刪掉 + 重新 reduce）。 If we commit to arc i→j, then city i's exit is decided (so delete row i) and city j's entry is decided (so delete column j). We also set cell (j,i) to ∞ to forbid the subtour i→j→i that would close too early. An include starts by inheriting the parent's bound, then we re-reduce the smaller matrix; if some surviving row or column has lost its only zero, it must be reduced again, and that extra amount is added on. Worked for "with 4→6": delete row 4 and column 6, block 6→4. Now look at row 5 — it used to reach a 0 via 5→6, but column 6 is gone, so its cheapest remaining exit is 5→1 = 3. Subtract that 3; no other row/column needs it. So the include bound = 96 + 3 = 99.如果我們決定用 arc i→j，那城市 i 的出口就定了（所以刪掉第 i 列），城市 j 的入口也定了（所以刪掉第 j 行）。我們還要把 (j,i) 設成 ∞，來禁掉 subtour i→j→i（不然會太早閉合成小圈）。一個 include 一開始先繼承父節點的下界，然後我們對縮小後的矩陣重新 reduce；如果某個還活著的列或行剛好失去了它唯一的 0，就得再 reduce 一次，多出來的量加上去。以「with 4→6」為例：刪掉第 4 列和第 6 行，封掉 6→4。現在看第 5 列——它本來靠 5→6 有個 0，但第 6 行被刪了，所以它剩下最便宜的出口是 5→1 = 3。減掉這 3；其他列／行都不用再減。所以 include 的下界 = 96 + 3 = 99。

So a "with" node's bound can change between frames — on purpose.所以同一個「with」節點的下界，在不同 frame 之間會變——這是故意的。 When the split first appears, the include side just inherits the parent bound (96), because we haven't re-reduced yet. The next step does the re-reduction, which can only raise it — here 96 → 99. That's why Frame 1 shows the "with 4-6" node at 96 and Frame 2 shows the same node at 99: not an error, just the second stage of bounding. Every include bound on the spine (99, 112, 126) is produced the same way: inherit, then re-reduce the shrunken matrix, and a freshly zero-less row/column bumps the bound up.當這個分裂第一次出現時，include 那邊只是繼承父節點的下界（96），因為我們還沒重新 reduce。下一步才做 re-reduction，而它只會把下界往上推——這裡是 96 → 99。所以 Frame 1 把「with 4-6」標成 96、Frame 2 把同一個節點標成 99：不是寫錯，只是 bounding 的第二階段。主幹上每個 include 的下界（99、112、126）都是這樣來的：先繼承，再對縮小的矩陣重新 reduce，剛失去 0 的那一列／行就把下界頂上去。

The same two rules generate every other number in the tree. Going down the spine, each new committed arc deletes another row and column; whenever the shrinking matrix loses a zero it gets re-reduced, which is why an include can still raise the bound (2→1 bumps 99→112, 1→4 bumps 112→126). And each exclude just adds its own penalty to the parent (without 3-5 → 117, without 2-1 → 125, without 1-4 → 153, without 6-7 → 141). You don't have to memorise the figures — you have the two rules, so any of them can be re-derived.樹裡其他每個數字都是這兩條規則生出來的。沿著主幹往下，每多選一條 arc 就再刪掉一列一行；只要縮小的矩陣掉了一個 0 就重新 reduce，這就是為什麼 include 也能把下界頂高（2→1 把 99→112、1→4 把 112→126）。而每個 exclude 就是把自己的 penalty 加到父節點上（without 3-5 → 117、without 2-1 → 125、without 1-4 → 153、without 6-7 → 141）。你不用去背這些數字——你有那兩條規則，任何一個都能自己推回來。

Honest caveat — the page doesn't draw every shrunken matrix. We only print one matrix in full (the 7×7 reduced one above). The deeper numbers (112, 126; the excludes 117, 125, 153, 141) come from matrices that get smaller at each step, and we describe rather than re-print them — so to truly verify them you'd redo the row/column deletions by hand. Here is the recipe, using the one fully-worked include (4→6 → 99) as your template: take the 7×7 reduced matrix, cross out row 4 and column 6, set (6,4)=∞, and you're left with a 6×6 grid; re-run the reduce step on it (the only line that lacked a zero, row 5, costs +3) to get 99. The very next branch repeats this on the 6×6: committing 3→5 means cross out row 3 and column 5, block (5,3)=∞, then re-reduce the 5×5 (here nothing new is forced, so it stays 99). Each level just shrinks by one row and one column and re-reduces — do it once by hand and the rest of the tree's numbers stop being mysterious.老實說一句——這頁沒把每張縮小後的矩陣都畫出來。我們只完整印了一張矩陣（上面那張 7×7 reduced）。後面比較深的數字（112、126；還有 excludes 117、125、153、141）是從一步步變小的矩陣算出來的，我們只用文字描述、沒重印——所以你要真的驗它們，得自己動手把列／行刪一遍。這裡給你食譜，拿那個唯一完整算過的 include（4→6 → 99）當範本：拿那張 7×7 reduced 矩陣，把第 4 列和第 6 行劃掉，設 (6,4)=∞，就剩一個 6×6；對它再跑一次 reduce（唯一沒有 0 的那行是第 5 列，要付 +3）就得到 99。下一個 branch 就在這個 6×6 上重複同一招：選 3→5 就劃掉第 3 列、第 5 行，封 (5,3)=∞，再 reduce 這個 5×5（這次沒有東西被新逼出來，所以維持 99）。每一層就是少一列、少一行再重新 reduce——你親手做過一次，樹裡剩下的那些數字就不再神祕了。

Near the end: why only one completion, and why "no solution".快到尾聲時：為什麼只剩一種走法，又為什麼會「no solution」。 Each committed arc fixes one city's exit and one city's entry. Once you've committed n−2 arcs, every row and column has at most one legal cell left, so the row/column "leave once, enter once" constraints leave exactly one way to close the tour — that's why "the remaining arcs are forced." And if you instead exclude the last arc the tour needs, some city now has no legal exit (or some city no legal entry): the matrix is infeasible, so that branch is (no solution) rather than just a higher bound.每選一條 arc，就固定了一個城市的出口和一個城市的入口。等你選滿 n−2 條 arc，每一列每一行最多只剩一個合法格子，於是「每個城市出去一次、進來一次」的限制就只剩一種方法能把 tour 封起來——這就是為什麼「剩下的 arc 是被逼著選的」。反過來，如果你把 tour 最後需要的那條 arc 排除掉，就會有某個城市沒有合法出口（或某個城市沒有合法入口）：矩陣無解，所以那條分支是 (no solution)，而不只是下界比較高而已。
