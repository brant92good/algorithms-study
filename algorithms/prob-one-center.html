1-Center — Smallest Enclosing Circle1-Center — 最小包覆圓

Where do you stand so that the farthest person is as close as possible? Find the centre of the smallest circle that covers every point.你要站在哪，才能讓離你最遠的那個人盡量靠近你？也就是找出那個把所有點都蓋住的最小圓的圓心。

🧠 Solved by:解法： Prune-and-Search

The problem這題在問什麼

You are given n points scattered on a flat plane — think of them as houses on a map. You must choose one location, the centre, and the cost of that choice is the distance to the point farthest from it. The 1-center problem asks: pick the centre that makes this farthest distance as small as possible.給你 n 個散在平面上的點——想成地圖上的房子。你要挑一個位置當圓心，而這個選擇的代價，就是離它最遠那個點的距離。1-center 問題要的是：挑一個圓心，讓這個最遠距離越小越好。

Equivalently — and this is the picture to hold onto — find the smallest circle that encloses all n points. The circle's centre is the answer; its radius is exactly that smallest-possible farthest distance. Every point lies inside or on the circle, and you can't make the circle any smaller without leaving a point out.換個等價的講法——也是最該記住的畫面——就是找出能把全部 n 個點都框進去的最小圓。這個圓的圓心就是答案；半徑剛好就是那個最小化後的最遠距離。每個點都在圓內或圓上，而且你只要再縮小一點點，就一定會漏掉某個點。

The key phrase is min-max: you minimise the maximum distance. Define the terms carefully:關鍵字是 min-max：你要最小化那個最大距離。把名詞講清楚：

  • Centre圓心 — the single point you get to place anywhere on the plane.——你可以擺在平面上任何地方的那一個點。
  • Radius半徑 — the distance from your centre to the farthest of the n points. This, and only this, is what you pay.——從圓心到 n 個點裡最遠那一個的距離。你付的代價就只有這個，別的都不算。
  • min-max — you are not minimising the total or the average distance. Only the single worst (largest) distance counts, because that worst point is what forces the circle to be big.——你不是在最小化總距離或平均距離。只有那個最糟（最大）的距離才算數，因為就是那個最糟的點逼著圓變大。

Where it's used — facility location.用在哪——設施選址。 If you must place one fire station, hospital, cell tower, or warehouse so that the worst-served customer is as close as possible, that is exactly the 1-center: nobody should be more than the radius away.如果你要蓋一座消防隊、醫院、基地台或倉庫，讓服務最差的那位客戶盡量靠近，這就正好是 1-center：沒有人應該離得比半徑還遠。

A tiny concrete instance.來個小例子。 Three points: A=(0,0), B=(4,0), C=(2,1). Standing at A makes the farthest point B, distance 4. Standing at the midpoint (2,0) drops the farthest distance to 2 (both A and B are exactly 2 away, and C is only ≈1.4 away). No spot does better — the smallest enclosing circle is centred at (2,0) with radius 2. Notice the answer is pinned by the two extreme points, not the middle one.三個點：A=(0,0)、B=(4,0)、C=(2,1)。站在 A，最遠的是 B，距離 4。站在中點 (2,0)，最遠距離就降到 2（A 和 B 都剛好 2 遠，C 只有約 1.4 遠）。沒有別的位置更好——最小包覆圓的圓心就在 (2,0)，半徑 2。注意：答案是被兩個最外側的點釘住的，不是中間那個。

How the solver works (prune-and-search).解法怎麼跑（prune-and-search）。 The slides solve 1-center in two stages. First the constrained version (§7.1): force the centre to lie on a given line, then find the best spot on that line in O(n). Then the general version (§7.2–7.3) calls that constrained solver repeatedly to home in on the true free centre, pruning a constant fraction of the points each round.投影片把 1-center 分兩階段解。先解 constrained（受限）版本（§7.1）：強迫圓心一定要落在某條給定的線上，然後在 O(n) 時間內找出那條線上最好的位置。接著 general（一般）版本（§7.2–7.3）就反覆呼叫這個受限解法，一步步逼近真正自由的圓心，每一輪都砍掉固定比例的點。

Why the sign test works.正負號測試為什麼有用。 After solving on a line, the algorithm asks "is the true free optimum above this line or below it?" Intuition: the centre wants to move in whatever direction shrinks the current worst distance. The farthest points pull like rubber bands — the combined pull of the binding farthest points points the way to move, and its sign (up vs. down) tells you which half-plane the optimum is in. That lets you throw away the other half.在線上解完之後，演算法會問：「真正自由的最佳圓心是在這條線上面、還是下面？」直覺是這樣：圓心會想往那個能把目前最糟距離變小的方向移動。最遠的那幾個點就像橡皮筋一樣在拉——這些卡住結果的最遠點合起來的拉力，就指出該往哪邊移，而它的正負號（往上還往下）就告訴你最佳解在哪半邊。這樣你就能把另外那半邊直接丟掉。

Why discarding the closer point of a pair is safe.為什麼丟掉每對裡比較近的那個是安全的。 In the constrained version the centre is pinned to a known line, so for any point you can read off how its distance changes as the centre slides. Pair the points up; for each pair, where their two distance curves cross splits the line into "point A is the farther one" on one side and "point B is the farther one" on the other. A median of those crossover positions tells you which side of the line the optimum sits on — and on that side, the closer point of each pair is provably never the binding farthest one, so it can be dropped with no effect on the answer. Each median split kills about a quarter of the points; doing it across both coordinate directions compounds to roughly ¼ × ¼ ≈ 1/16, which is where the ~n/16 pruned per round figure comes from.在受限版本裡，圓心被釘在一條已知的線上，所以對任何一個點，你都能看出圓心沿線滑動時它的距離怎麼變。把點兩兩配對；每一對的兩條距離曲線交叉的地方，會把這條線切成兩半——一半是「A 比較遠」，另一半是「B 比較遠」。取這些交叉位置的中位數，就能判斷最佳解落在線的哪一側；而在那一側，每一對裡比較近的那個點可以證明永遠不會是卡住結果的最遠點，所以丟掉它完全不影響答案。每次中位數切割大約砍掉四分之一的點；在兩個座標方向都做一次，疊起來大約是 ¼ × ¼ ≈ 1/16，這就是每輪砍掉 ~n/16 這個數字的由來。

Because a constant fraction (~1/16) vanishes every round, the surviving size shrinks geometrically: n → 15n/16 → (15/16)²n → …. The work follows T(n) = T(15n/16) + O(n), and summing that geometric series gives O(n) — linear time. Once the survivors drop to a tiny constant (the slides stop at ≤ 16 points), the algorithm just brute-forces the answer directly. Note: the exact 1/16 is the bound proved in the source; treat it as a quoted constant — the takeaway you must reconstruct is only that some fixed fraction is removed every round, which is what forces linearity.因為每一輪都消掉固定比例（約 1/16），活下來的數量是幾何級數縮小：n → 15n/16 → (15/16)²n → …。工作量滿足 T(n) = T(15n/16) + O(n)，把這個幾何級數加起來就是 O(n)——線性時間。當活下來的點少到只剩一個小常數（投影片是停在 ≤ 16 個點），演算法就直接暴力算出答案。注意：精確的 1/16 是來源裡證明出來的界限，把它當成一個引用的常數就好——你真正要會推的重點，只是每輪都會砍掉某個固定比例，這才是線性的關鍵。
