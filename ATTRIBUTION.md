## The course content — derived, educational, attributed
The chapter notes (`notes/`, `notes-html/`), the finals lessons (`lessons/`), and the few embedded figure images (`render/`) are **study material derived from a university Algorithms course**, itself based on:

> R. C. T. Lee, S. S. Tseng, R. C. Chang, Y. T. Tsai — *Introduction to the Design and Analysis of Algorithms.*

and the course lecture slides. The notes are a re-explained, re-organised study aid, **not** a copy of the slides; the original `.ppt`/`.pdf` lecture files are deliberately **not** included here. The embedded figures are the minimal set needed for the worked examples to stand on their own, reproduced for teaching and commentary.
